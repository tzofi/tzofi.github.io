I am a researcher at MIT Lincoln Laboratory where I study machine learning and computer vision. I will also be pursuing an M.S. in Data Science at Harvard University starting in Fall 2020, during which I will be advised by Hanspeter Pfister. My interests are in learning meaningful, robust, and interpretable representations of images. I am especially interested in applications of computer vision to medicine.

I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from The University of Alabama, where I worked with Travis Atkison. During my time at MIT Lincoln Laboratory, I have been mentored by Peter Morales.
