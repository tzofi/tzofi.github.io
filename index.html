About

I am a machine learning and software engineer at Amazon where I work on the natural language understanding behind Alexa. Formerly, I was a research engineer at MIT Lincoln Laboratory, where I studied machine learning and computer vision, and, in particular, their applications to medical images. My research interests are in learning meaningful, robust, and interpretable representations of data that enable low-resource learning.

I received my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from The University of Alabama, where I worked with Travis Atkison. I have also studied at MIT as an Advanced Studies Fellow.
